About Our Foundation

Nexa is a social enterprise building the foundations for a mentally sane Africa through arts and technology.

At Nexa, we believe in the potential of young people to charge ourselves and each other, to create an enabling environment for youth friendly mental health services. As architects of our future, young people can become advocates for mental wellbeing and sanity. We are rallying a community of mental health champions who are retelling the narratives of stigma and shame.

We are inspiring young people to be empathetic about the needs of each other, to be confident to seek help when they need to, and know that it is okay to not be okay sometimes.
